## About This Game

### Your Empire Evolves. Literally.

SwarmForge is a galactic strategy game where your productivity fuels your power. Choose one of three playable factions -- the Chitinid Swarm, the Infernal Legions, or the Eternal Dominion -- and carve your empire across 67,000 planets in a universe where idleness means extinction.

This is not another idle clicker with a space skin. SwarmForge combines deep OGame-style colony management with real-time tactical combat, a scientifically-grounded genetic evolution system, and a developer-controlled fourth faction that keeps you on your toes. Build your colonies, evolve your armies, and fight for survival in a galaxy that fights back.

### The GenomeForge: Evolution as a Weapon

At the heart of SwarmForge lies the GenomeForge -- a 64-gene genetic algorithm built on MAP-Elites and Novelty Search. Every creature in your army has a real genome. Breed them. Mutate them. Select for traits that counter your enemies. Facing armored targets? Evolve acid-spitting variants. Fighting in zero gravity? Evolve sealed carapaces with magnetic claws. Your army does not just grow -- it adapts.

The GenomeForge is not a gimmick. It is the core strategic layer. Two players with identical resources will field radically different armies because their evolutionary paths diverged. Your creatures are uniquely yours, shaped by your decisions and your battles.

### Four Factions, Four Philosophies

**The Chitinid Swarm** -- Billions of years of evolution produced a distributed intelligence that spans entire worlds. The Swarm has no individuals, only the collective. Overwhelm enemies with numbers and adapt to any threat through directed evolution. Spread Chitinous Resin across planets, turning every surface into sensory tissue that feeds intelligence back to the hive mind.

**The Infernal Legions** -- Demons from the Immaterium, a dimension of pure psychic energy. Each unit is individually devastating. Corrupt enemy territory with Hellfire, turning the battlefield itself into a weapon. But beware your own ambition -- every Demon Lord serves only themselves, and internal power struggles are as dangerous as external wars.

**The Eternal Dominion** -- Born from a failed immortality experiment, the undead armies of the Dominion do not stop. They do not retreat. They do not fear. Every enemy that falls in battle becomes a potential recruit. The Dominion's armies grow with every engagement, turning pyrrhic victories into strategic nightmares for their opponents.

**The Terran Imperium** (Developer-Controlled) -- Humanity is already here, already powerful, and already watching. The Imperium is controlled by the development team in real time, responding to player strategies, punishing complacency, and rewarding boldness. Think of it as a living difficulty system -- always present, always adapting, always just challenging enough to keep you honest.

### Build, Expand, Dominate

Colony management draws from the deep strategic tradition of OGame, updated with modern UI and quality-of-life features. Manage resource extraction, research technology trees, construct defenses, and expand across star systems. Every colony is a node in your empire's network -- lose a key node and watch supply chains collapse.

Nine galaxies. 499 systems each. 15 planet slots per system. The center is rich but deadly. The rim is safe but poor. Where you build matters as much as what you build.

### Combat That Rewards Skill

Battles use a LoL-inspired damage formula with 7 damage types and meaningful stat interactions. Positioning matters. Unit composition matters. Terrain matters. Bring a swarm of acid-spitters to a heavily armored fortress and watch the walls dissolve. Bring fire demons to a fungal world and watch the entire biome ignite.

Twelve Hero Champions lead your armies, each with unique abilities and upgrade paths. Champions are not disposable -- they grow stronger with experience, develop specializations, and become the lynchpins of your military strategy.

### Dark Matter: The Reward for Doing

The rarest resource in SwarmForge cannot be mined, bought, or stolen. Dark Matter generates only through productivity -- building, researching, evolving, creating. Idle empires produce none. Active empires accumulate the substance needed for Prestige Metamorphosis, ultimate abilities, and galaxy-shaping events. SwarmForge rewards players who play.

### Your Colony Never Sleeps

Log off and your empire keeps working. Offline idle progression means your colonies continue producing resources, your research continues advancing, and your creatures continue breeding. Come back to a stronger empire than the one you left. SwarmForge respects your time -- both the time you spend playing and the time you spend away.

### Why SwarmForge?

Because strategy games forgot what made them great. They forgot that depth is not complexity. They forgot that respecting the player means no predatory monetization. They forgot that a game should work without an internet connection.

SwarmForge remembers. One price. No microtransactions. No always-online requirements. No pay-to-win. Just a deep, evolving strategy game that rewards intelligence, creativity, and the willingness to keep building.

Your swarm is waiting. Forge it.

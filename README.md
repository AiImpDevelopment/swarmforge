## About

SwarmForge is a galactic strategy game where **your productivity fuels your power**. Choose your faction, colonize 67,000 planets across 9 galaxies, evolve your armies with a scientifically-grounded genetic algorithm, and wage war in a universe where idleness means extinction.

This is not another idle clicker with a space skin. SwarmForge combines **deep OGame-style colony management** with **real-time tactical combat**, a **64-gene genetic evolution system**, and a **developer-controlled fourth faction** that keeps you on your toes.

> **One price. No microtransactions. No battle pass. No premium currency. No pay-to-win.**

---

## Factions

### The Chitinid Swarm
*Billions of years of evolution produced a distributed intelligence that spans entire worlds.*

The Swarm has no individuals, only the collective. Overwhelm enemies with numbers and adapt to any threat through directed evolution. Spread **Chitinous Resin** across planets, turning every surface into sensory tissue that feeds intelligence back to the hive mind.

**Playstyle:** Swarm tactics, rapid evolution, biological terrain control.

### The Infernal Legions
*Demons from the Immaterium, a dimension of pure psychic energy.*

Each unit is individually devastating. Corrupt enemy territory with **Hellfire**, turning the battlefield itself into a weapon. But beware your own ambition — every Demon Lord serves only themselves, and internal power struggles are as dangerous as external wars.

**Playstyle:** Elite units, territory corruption, high-risk/high-reward.

### The Eternal Dominion
*Born from a failed immortality experiment.*

The undead armies of the Dominion do not stop. They do not retreat. They do not fear. Every enemy that falls becomes a potential recruit. The Dominion's armies **grow with every engagement**, turning pyrrhic victories into strategic nightmares.

**Playstyle:** Attrition, necromancy recruitment, relentless expansion.

### The Terran Imperium (Developer-Controlled)
*Humanity is already here, already powerful, and already watching.*

The Imperium is controlled by the development team in real time, responding to player strategies, punishing complacency, and rewarding boldness. Think of it as a **living difficulty system** — always present, always adapting, always just challenging enough.

**Playstyle:** Adaptive AI faction, dynamic difficulty, keeps the galaxy dangerous.

## GenomeForge

At the heart of SwarmForge lies the **GenomeForge** — a 64-gene genetic algorithm built on **MAP-Elites** and **Novelty Search**.

Every creature in your army has a **real genome**. Breed them. Mutate them. Select for traits that counter your enemies.

- Facing armored targets? **Evolve acid-spitting variants**
- Fighting in zero gravity? **Evolve sealed carapaces with magnetic claws**
- Need siege units? **Breed for explosive volatile glands**

Two players with identical resources will field **radically different armies** because their evolutionary paths diverged. Your creatures are uniquely yours, shaped by your decisions and your battles.

### How It Works

```
[Parent A] + [Parent B]
       |
   Crossover (64 genes)
       |
   Mutation (adaptive rate)
       |
   Fitness Evaluation
       |
   MAP-Elites Selection
       |
   [Offspring] → Your unique army
```

**15 Unit Tiers** from basic drones to galaxy-ending behemoths. **3 Prestige Metamorphosis tiers** for endgame colony transformation.

## Features

### Colony Building
- **9 Galaxies**, 499 systems each, 15 planet slots per system
- **67,000+ procedurally generated planets** (Voronoi, Perlin noise, Wave Function Collapse)
- **88 building types** with upgrade trees and supply chain dependencies
- **80 research technologies** across military, economic, and evolutionary branches
- Center is rich but deadly. Rim is safe but poor. **Where you build matters.**

### Combat
- **LoL-inspired damage formula** with 7 damage types and meaningful stat interactions
- **80 unit types** across 4 factions with unique abilities
- **12 Hero Champions** with experience growth, specializations, and upgrade paths
- **7 battle formations** for tactical advantage
- **SC2-style unit movement** with patrol systems and rally points

### Dark Matter Economy
The rarest resource in SwarmForge **cannot be mined, bought, or stolen**. Dark Matter generates only through productivity — building, researching, evolving, creating. Idle empires produce none. Active empires accumulate the substance needed for ultimate abilities and galaxy-shaping events.

**SwarmForge rewards players who play.**

### Offline Idle Progression
Log off and your empire keeps working. Colonies produce resources, research advances, creatures breed. Come back to a stronger empire. **SwarmForge respects your time.**

### Multiplayer
- **P2P Architecture** — no central server dependency
- **Alliance & Diplomacy** — trade agreements, espionage, betrayal
- **Delta-compressed state streaming** — low bandwidth, high fidelity
- **Optional** — fully playable solo offline

### Technical
- **20Hz fixed-rate tick simulation** — smooth, deterministic game state
- **18-table game database** — SQLite, WAL mode, no external dependencies
- **Delta-compressed streaming** — efficient multiplayer state sync
- **Cross-platform** — Linux, Windows, macOS (native desktop, not browser)

## Lore

The galaxy is old. Older than the light of its youngest stars. Three civilizations rose from the void — the Chitinid Swarm, shaped by billions of years of evolution; the Infernal Legions, born from the psychic Immaterium; and the Eternal Dominion, forged from a failed immortality experiment. Now humanity — the Terran Imperium — watches from its fortified systems, responding to threats with overwhelming technological force.

The war is not about territory. It is about survival. Every faction knows that in a galaxy of four apex predators, only one can dominate. The others will be consumed, corrupted, or converted.

**Your swarm is waiting. Forge it.**
